## High-Low

Spend a bet to roll a die (1–6). Guess whether the next roll will be **Higher** or **Lower**.

- **Win** — pot grows to 1.8× its current value
- **Tie** — push; pot is returned
- **Lose** — pot is lost

After a win you can **Roll Again** (the full pot rides on the next guess) or **Cash Out** to bank your winnings.

## Card Flip

Three cards are placed face-down; one is the Ace. Pick a card — if it is the Ace you win **2.5×** your bet. The other two cards are revealed after your pick.
